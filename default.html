Welcome to the Pantech Open Source Release Center

Pantech provides mobile devices, mainly smartphones, feature phones and portable multimedia players to make your life more convenient and productive. In order to better meet customers’ needs and wants, we use software of quality from Open Source Communities.

You can get free access to download Open Source Codes we adopted for our products at Pantech Open Source Release Center. We welcome your contacts for any questions and ideas you may have.
